INTERNET CONNECTION


  • Check your internet speed- https://www.speedtest.net/ (global speed-test site).
  • If your ping is more than 50-60 ms, there is some issue with the connectivity of your phone.
  • If the download and upload speeds are less the 4-5 mbps, your net connection is being compromised.
  • If any of these things happen, connect to Wi-fii if you are using your mobile data. Connect to mobile data if you are using Wi-fi.
